WE ARE ILITE.
ILITE, Inspiring Leaders in Technology and Engineering, is an organization that strives to inspire leaders in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). ILITE works in three branches: Robotics, Cyber Defense, and Sustainability. Our members participate in the FIRST® Robotics Competition, FIRST® Tech Challenge, Cyber Patriot, in addition to classroom sustainability initiatives. ILITE participates in outreach at the local, national, and international levels that has collectively inspired over 48,700 minds, as of our 2015 season.
